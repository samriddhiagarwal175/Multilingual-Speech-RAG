By his resurrection, Christ won the victory over law, sin, flesh, world, devil, death, hell, and every evil. Verse 2, and all the brethren which are with me, this should go far in shedding the mouths of the false apostles. Although the brethren with me are not apostles like myself, yet they are all of one mind with me, think, right, and teach as I do. They do not go where the enemies of the gospel predominate. They go where the Christians are. Why did they not invade the Catholic provinces and preach their doctrine to Godless frances, bishops, and doctors as we have done by the help of God? We look for that reward which I have not seen nor ear heard, neither have entered into the heart of man. Not all the Galatians had become perverted. means cannot be contaminated.
