Oh yes, Jack, and I'm nowhere, but I mean to have my innings before long. Of what Mrs. Neverben had gone through in providing birds, beasts and fishes, not to talk of tarts and jellies for the dinner of that day, no one but myself can have any idea. But it must be admitted that she accomplished her task with thorough success. We sat with the officer some little time after dinner and then went ashore. How much of evil, a real accomplished evil, had there not occurred to me during the last few days? What could I do now but just lay myself down and die? And the death of which I dreamt could not alas. When this captain should have taken himself and his vessel back to England, I would retire to a small farm, which I possessed at the furthest side of the island, and their inseclusion would end my days. Jack would become Eva's happy husband, and would remain amidst the hurried duties of the the eager world.
