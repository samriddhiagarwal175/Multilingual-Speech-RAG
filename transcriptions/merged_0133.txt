Fourth with all rant the opening of the tent to see what might be a miss, but Master Will, who peeped out first, needed no more than one glance. He gave way to the others very readily and retreated unpreceived by the squire and mistress Fitz-Euth to the rear of the tent. Cries of unnotting him, unnotting him! Before them fled the stroller and his three sons, capless and terrified. What is that tumult and rioting, cried out the squire, theoretically, and he blew twice on the silver whistle, which hung it as belt. Nay, we refuse their request most politely, most noble said the little stroller, and then they became vexed and would have snatched your purse from us. I could not see my boy injured excellence for but doing his duty as one of Cumberland's sons, so I did push this fellow. It is enough, said George Gamewell sharply, as he turned upon the crowd.
