The trunks were moved, the tree was pulled out and thrown. Rather hard it is true down on the floor, but a man drew him towards the stairs where the daylight shown. But it was not the third tree that they meant. It was in a corner that he lay among weeds and nettles. The golden star of tinsel was still on the top of the tree and glittered in the sunshine. In the court yards, some of the marriage children were playing who had danced at Christmas round the third tree, and were so glad at the side of him, and the gardener's boy chopped the tree into small pieces. There was a whole heap lying there. The wood flamed up splendidly under the large brewing copper, and its side so deeply. However, that was over now. the tree gone, the story had an end. Yes, dead these four years, and a good job for her too. Well, as I say, it's an awful queer world. They clap all the burglars and jell and the murderers and the wife beaters. I all are thought a general reproof would be enough punishment for a wife beater, because he probably has a lot of provocation that nobody knows. And the fire bugs can't think of the right name something like sendy erries. And the breakers of the peace and whatnot, and yet the law has nothing to say to a man like Hand Lord.
