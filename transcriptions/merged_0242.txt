Your majesty's plan then, in this affair, is you will take them from my private treasure. The news circulated with the rapidity of lightning. During its progress, it kindled every variety of coca-tree, desire and wild ambition. The king had completed his toilet by 9 o'clock. He appeared in an open carriage decorated with branches of trees and flowers. The queens had taken their seats upon a magnificent dais or platform erected upon the borders of the lake in a theater of wonderful elegance of construction. Suddenly, for the purpose of restoring peace in order, spring accompanied by his whole court made his appearance. The seasons, allies of spring, followed him closely to form a quadrile, which, after many words of more or less flattering import, was the commencement of the dance.
