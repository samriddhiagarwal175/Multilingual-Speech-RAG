Salam marveled and desired to be informed of the particulars. 9,000 years have elapsed since she founded yours, and 8,000 since she founded ours as our annals record. Many laws exist among us, which are the counterpart of yours as they were in the olden time. I will briefly describe them to you, initial read the account of them at your leisure in the sacred registers. Observe again what cared a lot took in the pursuit of wisdom, searching out the deep things of the world, and applying them to the use of men. The most famous of them all was the overthrow of the island of Atlantis. For at the peril of her own existence, and when the other hellians had deserted her, she repel the invader, and offer own accord gave liberty to all the nations within the pillars. This is the explanation of the shallows which are found in that part of the Atlantic Ocean. But I would not speak at the time because I wanted to refresh my memory. Then now let me explain to you the order of our entertainment. First, Timeyus, who is a natural philosopher, who speaks of the origin of the world, going down to the creation of men. And then I shall receive the men whom he has created, and some of whom will have been educated by you and introduce them to you as the last Athelian citizens of whom the Egyptian records spoke.
