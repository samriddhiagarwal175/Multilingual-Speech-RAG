The horizon seems extremely distant. All my danger and sufferings were needed to strike a spark of human feeling out of him. But now that I am well, his nature has resumed its sway. You seem anxious, my uncle. I said, seeing him continually with his glass to his eye. Angels! One might be, with less reason than now, I am not complaining that the rate is slow, but that the seat is so wide. We are losing time, and the fact is, I have not come all this way to take a little sail upon a pond on a raft. He called this sea upon, and I longed the way to take a little sail. Therefore don't talk to me about views and prospects. I take this as my answer, and I leave the professor to bite his lips with impatience. Sunday, August 16th.
