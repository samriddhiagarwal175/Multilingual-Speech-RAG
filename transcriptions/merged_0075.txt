A voice from beyond the world was calling. Hello, Stefanos! Here comes the dead-less! Their diving stone poised on its rude supports and rocking under their plunges, and the rough-hune stones of the sloping breakwater over which they scrambled in their horse play, gleamed with cold wet luster. He stood still in difference to their calls and parried their banter with easy words. It was a pain to see them, and a sword-like pain to see the signs of adolescence that made repellent their pitiful nakedness. Steffanos de los! A moment before, the ghost of the ancient kingdom of the Danes had looked forth through the vester of the He's wrapped city.
